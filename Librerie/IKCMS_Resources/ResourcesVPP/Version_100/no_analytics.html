This page was created in order to solve the problem of unwanted traffic data generated from site owners and developers.

Since we want to monitor our visitors' behaviour and not ourselves' in visiting the site, we are excluding our computers from being monitored by Google Analytics tool.
This solution is described by Google itself.
The computer which visits this page is excluded from the analytics monitoring till the cookies on it are deleted.
This page is not being crawled by search engines.

If you stumbled here by accident please report that to us and enjoy the site!
